Eggs

Description

Perfectly scrambled, salted and peppered eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Crack open and stir eggs in a separate container
  2. Strike pan over medium-low heat with butter
  3. Pour eggs onto pan and stir until cooked, but before browning (about 30-60 seconds)
  4. Place on plate and salt and pepper to taste
