Ribeye

Description

Perfectly cooked, reverse-seared, salted and peppered ribeye

Ingredients

  • 1 USDA Prime ribeye
  • Kosher salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Cast iron skillet

Steps

  1. 1 Hour before cooking, salt and pepper the steak thoroughly
  2. Preheat oven to 250 degrees F
  3. Cook steak in oven until desired cook level is reached
  4. Sear steak for 1 minute each side (including sides) on hot cast iron skillet
  5. Let steak sit for 5 minutes and then enjoy
